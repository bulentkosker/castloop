About Castloop

We built the streaming platform we always wanted — simple, reliable, and honest.

Our Story

Castloop was born out of frustration. As content creators ourselves, we spent hours setting up OBS, babysitting streams, and watching them crash at 3am. The existing tools were either too complex, too expensive, or simply unreliable.

We believed there had to be a better way. A platform that just works — where you upload your video, hit start, and it streams forever without you having to think about it.

That's Castloop. A global streaming platform built for creators who want to be always-on without the headaches.
